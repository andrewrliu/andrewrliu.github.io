About Me

I graduated cum laude from UCLA HSSEAS in Dec 2022 with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering. For the first three summers of undergrad, I interned at NASA JPL where I worked on NISAR, EMIT, and MSR.

Unknowingly, I discovered my passion for finance as a result of my newfound free time during quarantine. It started with using options to hedge my personal portfolio but continued with self-studying more traditional finance topics. Some teachings I found most valuable include MIT Professor Lo's Financial Theory I and NYU Professor Damodaran's Valuation + Corporate Finance courses.

Most recently, I interned at Wedbush as a TMT investment banking analyst the summer after my senior year. Currently, I am based in San Francisco working at Cambridge Associates as an investment analyst.

In my free time, you'll probably find me optimizing credit card rewards, collecting fragrances, cooking new recipes, playing basketball/golf, or hiking/traveling.
